Twilight Void Chronicles

 

The resulting product of this project is an action video game that blends mechanics and features typical of the genre with elements from role-playing and adventure games. Developed using Unity, the game aims to fulfill the universal human need for entertainment and is designed to be compatible with any household platform, enhancing its accessibility.

In this game, the user takes on the role of the main character, allowing them to interact with the game world and its various elements. As the player progresses, they face challenges in the form of enemies and puzzles, gradually improving their skills and enhancing the character’s attributes. The player also acquires items that boost the character’s abilities. This gameplay approach allows each player's experience to be personalized as they advance through the game.
