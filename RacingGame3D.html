Racing Game

Unity 3D

Description:

This demo, developed as part of the PAC 1 project for the UOC Masterâ€™s program, marks my first experience with the Unity 3D engine. The game features a car racing circuit where players take control of a vehicle to complete laps. The best lap time is recorded, and a ghost car is generated to visually represent the player's previous performance, allowing for real-time comparison and improvement in subsequent laps.

Instructions to play:

The controls may vary depending on the input device used. Since the game makes use of Standard Assets for the car, it includes scripts that allow adaptation to different input devices. However, when using a keyboard, the controls are as follows:

  • Move to the right: D or RightArrow.
  • Move to the left: A or LeftArrow
  • Accelerate: W or UpArrow.
  • Brake/Reverse: S or DownArrow
  • Pause: ESC
